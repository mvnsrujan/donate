About

The initiative is to improve the educational needs of poor children. The specific objectives of the program are to initiate the community to guide for support and education of poor children, to increase the enrollment of students in colleges, to increase the access of poor children to education, to change the life condition of poor students through education and to empower the households of poor students economically.

This initiative is taken, considering the poor and those who aren't getting their minimum need of requirement for their studies, to move further with a step-ahead.
